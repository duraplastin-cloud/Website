About Duraplast

Reliable plastic packaging, built with experience.

Duraplast is a nationwide manufacturer and supplier of molded plastic bottles, containers and jerry cans. With decades of experience in plastics manufacturing, we focus on production‑friendly designs and cost‑effective packaging solutions.

What we do

  • Manufacture molded plastic bottles, containers and jerry cans
  • Support multiple industries: pharmaceutical, chemical, lubricant, food, cosmetics, edible oil, agro, dairy and more
  • Help clients select the right packaging based on filling volume, closure and handling needs

Why clients choose us

  • Product design and technical expertise built over years
  • Durable, leak‑proof and tamper‑option packaging
  • Recyclable material options and production consistency
